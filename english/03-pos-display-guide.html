DISPLAY DO'S AND DON'TS

‚úÖ DO

  • Keep displays fully stocked - empty spots reduce perceived value
  • Face products forward with labels clearly visible
  • Place at eye level whenever possible
  • Use clean, professional signage
  • Rotate older stock to front (FIFO)
  • Keep product dust-free and boxes pristine
  • Position near complementary products
  • Train all staff on product benefits
  • Update signage for seasonal promotions
  • Create visual contrast with shelf background

‚ùå DON'T

  • Hide behind cheaper commodity products
  • Place on bottom shelf (reduced visibility)
  • Mix with non-pet or unrelated items
  • Use damaged or outdated signage
  • Allow displays to get dusty or disorganized
  • Block products with promotional materials
  • Position in low-traffic corners
  • Forget to restock after busy weekends
  • Use handwritten or unprofessional signs
  • Place near strong-smelling products
