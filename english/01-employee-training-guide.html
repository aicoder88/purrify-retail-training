1. PRODUCT KNOWLEDGE

What is Purrify?

Purrify is a premium activated coconut carbon odor eliminator designed specifically for cat litter boxes. It provides chemical-free, natural odor control that keeps homes fresh for up to 7 days.

How Activated Coconut Carbon Works

The Science Made Simple:

  • Porous Structure: Coconut carbon has millions of tiny pores that trap odor molecules like a sponge
  • Activation Process: Heat treatment creates an ultra-porous surface with massive absorption capacity
  • Odor Elimination: Unlike air fresheners that mask smells, Purrify actually captures and neutralizes odor molecules
  • Safe & Natural: No chemicals, fragrances, or artificial ingredients

5. FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Q: How much should I use?

A: For a standard litter box, sprinkle approximately 2-3 tablespoons evenly across the top of clean litter, then mix gently.

Q: How often do I need to reapply?

A: Reapply every 7 days, or when you completely change the litter.

Q: Can I use this with automatic litter boxes?

A: Yes! Purrify works with all types of litter boxes, including automatic and self-cleaning models.

Q: Is it safe for kittens?

A: Absolutely! Purrify is 100% non-toxic and safe for cats of all ages.

Q: What if I have multiple cats?

A: Purrify works great in multi-cat households! For more than 2 cats, consider using slightly more product or reapplying every 5-6 days.

Q: Will it change the texture of the litter?

A: No, the carbon granules are designed to blend seamlessly with litter without changing its texture or clumping properties.

Q: Can I use it with litter deodorizers or baking soda?

A: Yes, but most customers find they don't need additional products once they start using Purrify. It's more effective on its own.

Q: What's the shelf life?

A: When stored in a cool, dry place with the container sealed, Purrify maintains its effectiveness for 2+ years.

Q: Is it environmentally friendly?

A: Yes! Made from renewable coconut sources, fully biodegradable, and packaged in recyclable materials.

Q: What if it doesn't work for me?

A: We stand behind our product with a satisfaction guarantee. If you're not happy, we'll make it right.
